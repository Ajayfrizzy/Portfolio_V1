About Me

Hi! I'm Oluwaseun Ajao, a passionate Software Engineer with a love for creating functional and beautiful web experiences. With over 12 months of experience in the industry, I've had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects that have helped me grow both technically and creatively.
